There is a lot of HATE in this world. If we face the war in Ukraine, if we talk about BIPOC and discrimination in Germany or if we talk about the queer community.

At DCI we have been thinking a lot, how can we stop the hate and turn the energy into something positive?

The answer is: let's create together. And where can we build bridges easier than in technology? Where we can connect and collaborate beyond borders, beyond sexual orientation, religion or ethnicities.

Let's focus on creating something positive together and shape the world through digital transformation. Information Technology can help to make the world a better place.

At DCI everyone is welcome

Since our foundation in 2016 we have a mission: We want to help people find a secure job in Germany.

It is not important to us what your background is, your skin color or who you love. We only care about the way you think: be open minded, be brave and ready for a change. To take your future into your own hands and think technology anew.

For that we offer many different courses such as Web Development, Online Marketing, Programming and Human Resources. There is something for everyone!

So what are you waiting for: Take a look at our courses and start your new path.
