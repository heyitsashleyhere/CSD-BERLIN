Our courses are for everyone.

All our courses are certified by the Agentur für Arbeit and are regularly checked for their quality. We want to offer you the best training possible and prepare you perfectly for your future career. That's why we have a particularly high practical component in our courses and focus on real-world problems. We also don't let you down when it comes to job applications: Our Career Success Team prepares you intensively for future job interviews.

All our courses are free!

We are convinced that everyone should have the same chances and opportunities regardless of their gender, age or background. At DCI we support each other to become the best version of ourselves. To give everyone an equal start all our courses can be funded by a Bildungsgutschein (education voucher) from the Agentur für Arbeit or the Jobcenter. That means: They cover the full costs, you don’t have to pay anything and later do not have to pay it back either. For you it is 100% free of charge. More to that you can learn on our course page.
